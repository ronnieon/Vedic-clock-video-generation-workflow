The Tandava began. As each of Kaliya's other heads rose to strike, Krishna's feet met it 
with a rhythmic, crushing stomp. He danced from head to head, a whirlwind of gold and 
indigo, his feet stamping out the serpent's pride with every perfect step. "You have many 
heads, Kaliya," Krishna's voice called out. "Do they all know how to bow?"
