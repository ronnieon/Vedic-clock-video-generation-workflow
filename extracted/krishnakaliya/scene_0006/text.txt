A brilliant light began to pulse from within Kaliya's grip. It grew from a soft hum to an 
incandescent blaze. With a mighty groan, Krishna expanded his divine form. The 
serpent's coils, which had held him fast, stretched and strained until they burst apart with 
a deafening crack. "GAAH! WHAT ARE YOU?!" Kaliya roared, his body recoiling in pain 
and shock as Krishna floated effortlessly above the churning water.
