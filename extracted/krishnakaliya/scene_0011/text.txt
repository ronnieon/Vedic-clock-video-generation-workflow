One by one, Kaliya's heads were beaten, bloodied, and forced down. The great serpent, 
vomiting blood and poison, began to sink under the impossible weight of the small boy 
on his head. "You wanted to be King of the River," Krishna's voice echoed, calm amidst 
the fury. "Can you bear the weight of the Universe?"
