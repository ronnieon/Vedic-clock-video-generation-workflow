Before Krishna could answer, Kaliya struck. The great serpent lunged, wrapping the boy 
in the crushing grip of his coils. The Bhogi-Bandhan, the serpent-trap, tightened, strong 
enough to shatter mountains. On the shore, the people of Vrindavan cried out in terror. 
But within the prison of scales, Krishna was unbothered. This hug is a bit tight, he 
thought to himself. I should stop playing dead.
